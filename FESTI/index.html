What is FESTI?
FESTI is a student/local vendor social event
and an exciting platform to get to know each other through parties.
It gives you the opportunity to explore the local nightlife scene.

Event Details
The Student-Bar Connection Event will be a one-day extravaganza held outside of iCLA easily accessible to both students and local bars.
The event will feature a range of interactive activities, networking opportunities, and entertainment, ensuring an engaging and memorable experience for all attendees.
Objective and
Event Highlights
Promote student connections by having student-only or similar parties.
Also, revitalize local bars specific to the area and the community participation that goes along with it.
The project also includes live performances, networking, and promotion during the parties.

Potential Collaboration

Potential Collaboration refers to the Izakaya and Bar that FESTI is currently considering.
FESTI is considering a variety of Potential Collaborations, and is constantly evolving to keep customers happy and entertained.。
